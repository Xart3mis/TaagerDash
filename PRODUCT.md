# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Internal Taager media buyers and admins. They open this tool at the start of their day, check campaign performance across Meta, TikTok, and Snapchat, log funnel data, review targets, and compare results on the leaderboard. They are comfortable with marketing metrics (CTR, ROAS, CPA, AOV) and come here to work quickly, not to be onboarded. Admins additionally manage team access via invite tokens.

## Product Purpose

A private, invite-only ad performance dashboard for the Taager media buying team. It centralizes cross-platform ad metrics, derived KPIs, order funnel data, and per-user performance benchmarks. Success means a buyer can open the dashboard, read their numbers, spot what is off, and act, all within 60 seconds.

## Brand Personality

Friendly, approachable, clear. The tool should feel like a well-organized shared workspace run by people who care about clarity and their teammates' time. Warm enough that daily use feels comfortable, precise enough that numbers read immediately. Not cool or slick for its own sake.

## Anti-references

- Dark terminal and developer-tool aesthetics: wrong energy for a media buying team
- Heavy BI tools (Tableau, Looker): too corporate, too dense, too old-school
- Generic SaaS-cream dashboards: washed-out blues, excessive whitespace, forgettable

## Design Principles

1. **Read the number first.** Every screen's primary job is legibility. Hierarchy, spacing, and contrast exist to surface the key figure fast.
2. **Warm precision.** Friendliness and accuracy are not in tension. Approachable tone, careful typography, no coldness.
3. **Shared workspace energy.** Leaderboard, targets, and team roles are social features. The UI should feel like something built for a team, not a solo analytics tab.
4. **Earn density gradually.** The default view shows the signal. Detail lives one click deeper, not on first load.
5. **Calm confidence.** No anxiety-inducing red-heavy alerts, no aggressive gamification. Performance feedback is clear and direct, not punishing.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Standard contrast ratios, keyboard navigation, screen reader labeling on interactive elements. No animations that cannot be suppressed via `prefers-reduced-motion`.
